The Industry Challenge

Acquiring a customer has never been more expensive — or easier to get wrong.

Ad costs rise every year while attention spans shrink. A shopper decides in seconds, and a slow page, a confusing checkout, or a weak offer loses them permanently. Meanwhile the marketplace giants set the expectation for speed and convenience that every smaller brand is measured against.

The margin between profit and loss sits in details most brands never optimise: the conversion rate, the cost per acquisition, and — most decisively — whether a customer ever buys twice. Winning in ecommerce isn't about traffic. It's about what each visitor is worth, and how many come back.

FAQ
Which is the better investment — more traffic or better conversion?
Almost always conversion first. Doubling your conversion rate has the same effect as doubling your traffic, but costs a fraction as much. Once the funnel converts efficiently, every dirham of ad spend works harder.
How important is email for an ecommerce brand?
Very. Email is typically among the highest-returning channels because it's owned — no rising ad costs, no algorithm. Automated flows in particular generate revenue continuously once built.
Can you work with our platform — Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento?
Yes. We work across major ecommerce platforms and integrate with your existing stack rather than requiring a migration.
How quickly can we improve return on ad spend?
Restructuring campaigns and tightening targeting often shows improvement within weeks. Sustainable, profitable scale usually follows within one to three months, once creative and audience testing have produced clear winners.
