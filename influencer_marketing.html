The Problem

Big followings don't guarantee anything.

Brands overpay for reach that doesn't convert, partner with creators whose audiences don't match, and have no way of proving what the campaign returned. Meanwhile audiences have grown sharp at spotting inauthentic endorsements. Influencer marketing works when the fit is genuine, the brief is clear, and the results are tracked.

FAQ
How do you choose influencers?
Audience overlap with your customers, genuine engagement rates, content quality, and brand fit — verified with data, not follower counts.
Is influencer marketing only for B2C?
No. B2B influencer marketing through industry experts, analysts, and LinkedIn voices is increasingly effective.
How do you measure results?
Tracked links, discount codes, UTM parameters, and platform analytics — tied to reach, engagement, traffic, and sales.
What does a campaign cost?
It depends on creator tier and scope. Micro-influencer campaigns can start modestly; we'll build a plan to your budget.
