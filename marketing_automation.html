The Problem

Leads go cold while your team is busy elsewhere.

Prospects who aren't ready to buy today get forgotten tomorrow. Follow-up depends on someone remembering. Sales and marketing work from different data and argue about lead quality, because nobody can say which leads are genuinely worth calling.

The default answer is to hire more people to do more manual work. The better answer is to build systems that nurture every lead consistently, score them objectively, and hand sales only the ones that are ready — so growth doesn't depend on headcount.

Typically four to eight weeks depending on complexity and the number of integrations. We'll scope it precisely in the audit.
Will this replace our sales team?
No — it makes them significantly more effective, by handing them warmer, better-qualified, better-prioritised leads.
