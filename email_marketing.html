The Problem

Your list is an asset you're almost certainly under-using.

Most brands send the occasional newsletter to everyone on the list, hope for the best, and watch open rates quietly decline. There's no segmentation, no automation, and no clear link between sending and selling.

Yet email consistently delivers one of the strongest returns of any marketing channel — but only when the right message reaches the right person at the right moment. An unsegmented blast to a cold list isn't email marketing. It's noise, and your audience treats it accordingly.

My list is small — is email still worth it?
Yes. A small, engaged list routinely outperforms a large, cold one. We'll help you grow it ethically while making far more of the subscribers you already have.

Most major platforms — Klaviyo, Mailchimp, HubSpot, and others. We'll recommend what genuinely fits your business rather than forcing a migration you don't need.
How often should we email?
Often enough to stay present, not so often you're ignored. The right cadence depends on your audience, offer, and lifecycle stage — we'll define it in the audit.
Will you write and design the emails?
