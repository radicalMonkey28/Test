FAQ

Questions, answered.

Isn't link building risky?
Only when done badly. Buying links violates Google's guidelines and can bring penalties. We earn links through genuine coverage and useful assets, which is both safe and durable.
How many links will we get?
It varies by campaign and sector — we'll set realistic expectations in your audit. Quality matters far more than quantity: a handful from authoritative titles beats hundreds from low-value sites.
How is this different from traditional PR?
Traditional PR chases brand awareness; digital PR is engineered to earn links and citations that also lift your search rankings.
How long does a campaign take?
Typically six to twelve weeks from concept to coverage, with links continuing to arrive afterwards.
